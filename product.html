ABOUT THIS DELICACY

A timeless classic from the heart of Kerala! Avulose Podi is a meticulously roasted blend of fine rice flour and freshly grated coconut, seasoned with a hint of cumin and cardamom. Perfectly crunchy with an earthy aroma, it can be enjoyed as a tea-time snack mixed with sugar and plantains, or simply by itself. Handcrafted in small batches to preserve the authentic heritage flavor.
